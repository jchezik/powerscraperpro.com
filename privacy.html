Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Overview

Power Scraper Pro is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Data We Collect

Email Addresses

When you sign up for our launch notification waitlist, we collect your email address. This is used solely to notify you when Power Scraper Pro becomes available for purchase.

Chat Messages

Messages sent through our website's chat widget are processed by Anthropic Claude AI to provide customer support responses. These messages may contain questions about the product or technical support requests.

How We Use Your Data

  • Email addresses are used exclusively for launch announcements and product updates
  • Chat messages are processed by AI to provide relevant customer support
  • We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools that collect personal data

Third-Party Services

Anthropic Claude AI

Chat messages are sent to Anthropic Claude for AI-powered responses. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle this data. Messages are not stored permanently and are processed solely for the purpose of generating responses.

Cloudflare

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which may collect technical information such as IP addresses for security and performance purposes, governed by Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), if you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data
  • Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS protocols.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, your data, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

Email: powerscraperpro@gmail.com

We are based in Spain and comply with EU data protection regulations.
