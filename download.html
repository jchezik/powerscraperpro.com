FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with NAS drives?

Yes. Power Scraper Pro includes NAS resilience with automatic retry logic, volume disconnect detection, and operation queuing during disconnects.

Will it modify my original files?

With Safe Mode enabled, your original files are never touched. The app creates hardlinks in a clean library structure. Without Safe Mode, files are renamed in-place but can be restored to original names.

What file formats are supported?

MKV, MP4, AVI, M4V, MOV, WMV, FLV, WebM, TS, M2TS, MPG, MPEG, and more. Configurable in settings with minimum file size filtering.

Can I use it with multiple Macs?

Yes. iCloud Sync keeps your settings synchronized across all your Macs. API keys remain secure in each Mac's local Keychain.

What if a movie is matched incorrectly?

Use the Rename & Rescrape feature to manually enter the correct TMDB or TVDB ID. The correction is saved and applied to future scrapes.

Does it support Apple Silicon?

Yes. Power Scraper Pro is a universal binary running natively on both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs with Metal GPU acceleration.
